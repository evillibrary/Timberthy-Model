# This project is currently in two parts. The model builder and the cutting board. 

# The **model builder** is used to get a to-scale look at furniture you want to make (currently only the bookshelf that inspired this prject:)). You can
# manipulate the dimensions of the bookshelf and have the corresponding pieces adjust accordingly in a pieces list. You can also adjust the color of the furniture, 
# look at some scale objects next to it, and view the measurements on the model itself. The model will also warn you if your parameters are moving towards impractical/impossible.
# Needs more furniture types! How to do it? Bookshelf works well because it's simple and pieces are all symmterical!

# The cutting board is probably more practical and helps you get the most effecient use when cutting up boards/planks for use. It runs in cycles: choose your material and material thickness / 
# indicate what size boards are available / add desired pieces by dimensions and quantity / view a helpful diagram that lays out the best way to get all your pieces with minimum amount of waste.
# There is an additional cycle in production to allow you to mark off drill points on your pieces (needs work!) Results are downloaded either seperately per material or all together as a PDF.
