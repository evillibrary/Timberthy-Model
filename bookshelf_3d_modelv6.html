Design brief

A freestanding unit that sits up on a low plinth for a bit of lift off the floor. At the base is a closed cupboard, with two doors that open outward from the centre, hinged on the outer edges, with a handle on each inner edge where they meet. Above the cupboard, open shelves for books and display, evenly spaced. The wall behind the open shelves is finished — wallpapered or painted a solid colour — so it reads as a deliberate backdrop rather than bare board. Two solid side panels run the full height, capped by a slightly larger top panel that overhangs like a lid. Everything is left in raw pine so it can be stained or varnished to whatever colour is wanted.
